About

Providing spacious accommodation for up to 13 people, Villa Nuage is an imposing, contemporary,south facing 800m2 luxury French villa in a totally private, verdant setting with stunning, uninterrupted views of the Mediterranean Sea and Monaco, located under ten minutes drive from Monte Carlo and all of the magic it has to offer.

Nuage Villa

Extensively modernised with top-of-the range fixtures and fittings throughout 2021 and furnished to the very highest standards at the beginning of 2022 to provide a sumptuous Art Deco feel, Villa Nuage is available for the first time this year as a luxury holiday villa, one which occupies an enviable position on the Côte d'Azur on the outskirts of Monaco. With 800 square metres of accommodation (8,800 square feet) this beautifully finished four-bedroom holiday villa can comfortably accommodate up to 11 people, while there is an additional and delightful 30-square-metre guest house for two additional people or an au pair/nanny.

All bedrooms and principal reception rooms face south, and all enjoy magnificent triple-aspect views of nearby woodland, an uninterrupted picture-postcard view over the Mediterranean Sea, and vista across Monte Carlo. Each bedroom opens out onto a south-facing terrace above which are individual electrically operated awnings to provide welcome shade on the hottest of summer days. In addition, each bedroom has its own, private, luxurious en-suite shower room with Italian-style walk-in shower. Villa Nuage also comes fully automated with a ‘smart home system’ that control the curtains and shutters, underfloor heating (which is rarely needed), air conditioning, lights and multimedia, while there is also broadband internet connection.

Outside, beyond the large expanses of sun terraces, there is a substantial paved patio, ideal for a party or bar-b-que adjacent to the large, sculptured 68-square-metre swimming pool. The delightful gardens with manicured lawns, shrubberies and a wide variety of mature trees provide quiet corners where the verdant canopy of foliage provides welcome and natural shade, as well as providing a delightful walk late in the evening as the sun sets. There is ample parking within the grounds, while there is also a spacious six-car garage.

Formerly a private home, today, Villa Nuage occupies a hillside plot of approximately 3,870 square metres (just under an acre) of professionally landscaped grounds at the end of a private access road in Roquebrune, on the doorstep of the Principality of Monaco. The location is perfect for a private villa if you want to enjoy the vibrancy of the iconic town of Monte Carlo, which is less than ten minutes by car to the very centre, sea front and beaches, yet wish to retreat at times to somewhere more tranquil and peaceful to relax by the pool and enjoy the glorious sunshine of an enviable Mediterranean climate.

Just two kilometres and well out of earshot from the property is the A8 autoroute which gives easy access to the Italian border which is less than 12km distant to the east. The major city of Nice is 25km to the west, and the international Nice Côte d'Azur Airport 35km distant. If you have friends coming by train to visit, the nearest railway station is in the heart of Monte Carlo.

While the A8 gives you rapid access to much of the Côte d'Azur, it also gives you access to multiple minor roads that lead inland, away from the coast, to small towns and villages that will treat you to a more traditional form of French culture and which are well worth exploring. Villa Nuage’s location is ideal as you do not have to negotiate the busy streets of Monte Carlo whenever you want to go anywhere.

For those of you who would enjoy a morning stroll down to a French or, to be more accurate, Monegasque café to enjoy a strong, dark ‘tasse de café’ and croissant or (irresistible) pain au chocolat, there is a shortcut into the town from the property down narrow lanes which takes less than fifteen minutes to walk as it is all very much downhill.

Accommodation

The principal reception rooms and bedrooms occupy two floors of Villa Nuage, taking full advantage of the stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea and nearby Monte Carlo through full-height picture windows and patio doors. Aside from the summer kitchen, most rooms have solid oak parquet flooring which adds to the overall warmth of character of the villa. Sumptuous furniture adorns each and every room, nodding its head to the nuance of Art Deco throughout. A clever combination of fabric- and leather-upholstered furniture accentuate a sense of opulence, bolstered by the stunning chandeliers and impressive light fittings throughout.

Nuage Villa

At Villa Nuage we have tried to provide the ideal holiday retreat both for gathering socially, and also providing privacy and tranquility, some personal space, when needed. The large, richly furnished living room includes a 100-inch television and is flanked on the one side by the mirrored-wall games room and on the other by the magnificent reception hall and spacious dining room. The formal dining room, with its in-keeping Art Deco-style fireplace, has an imposing mahogany table which is set for 12, though there is additional seating and tabling to cater for up to 20 dinner guests. The games room is furnished with both foosball and a pool table. The magnificent, fully equipped kitchen also doubles up as a breakfast room or casual dining room with a table which seats six, comfortably. The kitchen comes with everything you, or the chef, could possibly need, and much more besides, including an essential coffee machine, wine cooler, and multiple refrigerators. In addition to the kitchen there is a second, summer kitchen and also a utility/laundry room with all the necessary white goods.

All four bedrooms are generously furnished with king-size beds, each room having its own, individual style. All bedrooms also have a shower room, which includes a Japanese Toto toilet and Italian-style walk-in ‘rainwater’ shower. Bathrobes, towels, hairdryers and toiletries are all included. The Master Bedroom includes an office, jacuzzi and sauna. There is extensive walk-in wardrobe space and ample plush, soft furnishings, and in three of the four bedrooms, should you have a surfeit of guests wanting to stay overnight, there is a discretely hidden drop-down single bed that, when not in use, folds away discretely into one of the cupboards. As with all the main reception rooms, each bedroom has a full-height patio door to take full advantage of the spectacular view down to the sea and Monte Carlo. All bedrooms are connected to Apple AirPlay, while two of the bedrooms have TVs connected to Apple TV and Kartina TV. Each bedroom has an individual wall-mounted safe.

Adjacent to the main property is a guest annexe that contains a double bedroom and en-suite shower room, which also enjoys the wonderful views of the Mediterranean. Inside the main house there is also the fully equipped gymnasium, massage room, hammam (steam room) and staff shower room.

Nighttime in Monaco

Who can resist the allure of the Casino de Monte Carlo, immortalised not once, but twice in James Bond films? Of course, there is so much more to Monte Carlo than a casino. Beyond the wonderful choice of restaurants, there is the Opéra Garnier Monte-Carlo which is attached to the Casino de Monte-Carlo. There is also the Grimaldi Forum, a popular venue for concerts, with Iggy Pop putting on a free show on May 19. The Theatre Princess Grace Monaco is a wonderful venue, while for something out of the ordinary, Le Fort Antoine dans le Ville is an open-air theatre which puts on productions throughout the summer months. If nightclubs are your scene, check out Blue Gin on the water’s edge which unbeatable views of the Mediterranean. La Rascasse is probably the most famous bend on the FI Monaco Grand Prix circuit, and it also happens to be the location of the top, eponymously named bar and nightclub. Jimmy’z is perhaps the most iconic nightclub in Monaco, and if you are a night owl, The Living Room doesn’t even open its doors before 11 p.m.!

Eating Out

Did you know that in 2020 there were 628 Michelin-starred restaurants in France, of which 86 were located in the Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur region, surrounding Monaco on all sides? The exciting news is that one of the very best restaurants in the country is located in Monte Carlo, Le Louis XV - Alain Ducasse à l'Hôtel de Paris , a three-star Michelin restaurant where chefs Dominique Lory and Alain Ducasse have created a modern and contemporary menu. There are six other Michelin-starred restaurants in Monaco, including Elsa, which specialises in Mediterranean cuisine, Yoshi, which is a Japanese restaurant, and La Table d’Antonio au Rampoldi, which delights with creative Italian cuisine. Maya Bay (Japanese and Thai), Song Qi (Asian and Chinese) and Beefbar (grilled meats) are all also mentioned in the Michelin Guide. If you want some insider knowledge regarding the best places to eat offering fresh local produce, have a quiet word with your executive chef!

Activities

Walking

Walking - Le Château Medieval de Roquebrune , set within the pretty medieval town, is located ten minutes to the east of Villa Nuage and is definitely worth visiting. There are some delightful walks around the town, which then provide a great excuse for stopping for lunch at La Grotte & L’Olivier or Restaurant Casarella.

Tennis

In 2022 the Rolex Monte-Carlo Masters is being held between April 9 and 17 at the Monte Carlo Country Club , which is less than five minutes from the villa. Of course, if you prefer playing rather than watching, there are plenty of courts available at the club to non-members to book for a game.

Golf

Monte Carlo Golf Club is 20 minutes away and the 18-hole golf course is open to non- members, though a tee time must be booked in advance. There is golf club hire, but a valid handicap certificate is required, with a maximum 35.

Boating and Watersports

A superb Axopar 37 motorboat is available for daily rent directly from the owners of Villa Nuage, which will give you the perfect opportunity to explore the Côte d'Azur from the sea or to find a quiet sandy beach to enjoy an al fresco lunch. Being on the Côte d'Azur and with the Mediterranean Sea at the foot of the hill, it is no surprise that watersports play a major role in local sporting activities. From jet skiing to water skiing at Monte Carlo Beach , everything water- related is literally just a few minutes away from Villa Nuage.
